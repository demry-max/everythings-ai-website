We're excited to announce the official launch of **Everything's AI** — the Philippines' first dedicated AI education and innovation company.

## Our Mission

Everything's AI exists to build the next generation of AI talent and elevate the Philippines' global competitiveness in the era of artificial intelligence.

## What We Offer

- **AI Incubator** — Build, compete, and get funded. Our Incubator is the launchpad for the Philippines' most ambitious AI builders and startups.
- **Academy Tutor** — Fun, structured, and forward-looking AI education for children ages 8–16.

Whether you're an AI builder with a big idea or a parent ready to invest in your child's future, Everything's AI has a place for you.
